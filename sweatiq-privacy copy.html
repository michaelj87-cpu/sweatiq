PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 24, 2025

SweatIQ is an AI-powered fitness coaching application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Messages you send to the AI coach — Your fitness questions, goals, and conversations with SweatIQ
  • Progress data — Measurements, photos, and saved workouts you choose to store (saved locally on your device)
  • Payment information — Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details
  • Device information — Basic technical data such as device type and operating system for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized AI fitness coaching responses
  • To process your subscription payments
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of our AI coaching
  • To communicate with you about your account

3. AI Service Disclosure

Important: SweatIQ uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate coaching responses. Your messages are transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data processing. SweatIQ does not sell your conversation data. We do not use your messages to train AI models without your consent.

4. Data Storage

  • Progress photos and measurements — Stored locally on your device only. We never upload your photos to our servers
  • Saved workouts and meal plans — Stored locally on your device via browser storage
  • Conversation history — Not permanently stored on our servers. Sessions are temporary
  • Account and payment data — Stored securely via Stripe

5. Third-Party Services

SweatIQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Processes your messages to generate coaching responses. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Processes payment information securely. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Railway — Hosts our backend server infrastructure
  • Netlify — Hosts our frontend application

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above that are necessary to operate SweatIQ.

7. Children's Privacy

SweatIQ is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Health Disclaimer

SweatIQ provides general fitness and nutrition information only. The AI coach is NOT a licensed medical professional, registered dietitian, or certified personal trainer. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting a new exercise program or making significant dietary changes. SweatIQ is not responsible for any health outcomes resulting from the use of this application.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete your local data (progress photos, measurements, saved items) at any time through the Progress page. To request deletion of your account and any associated data, email us at support@sweatiq.app and we will process your request within 30 days.

10. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@sweatiq.app

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, notifying you through the app.
