FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel your subscription at any time by emailing [email protected] with the subject "Cancel Subscription." If you subscribed through the App Store, you can cancel directly in your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SweatIQ. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.
How do I get a refund? +
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. Email [email protected] within 7 days of your first payment with the subject "Refund Request" and we'll process it within 5 business days. For App Store purchases, refunds are processed through Apple.
Is SweatIQ a real personal trainer? +
No. SweatIQ is an AI-powered coaching tool powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. It is not a licensed personal trainer, medical professional, or registered dietitian. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any new fitness program or making significant dietary changes.
What data does SweatIQ collect? +
SweatIQ transmits your messages to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses. Progress photos and measurements are stored locally on your device only — we never upload them to our servers. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my progress data? +
Your progress photos, measurements, and saved items are stored locally on your device. You can delete individual items from the Progress page, or clear all local data by going to your browser settings and clearing site data for SweatIQ.
What equipment do I need to use SweatIQ? +
None. SweatIQ builds programs around whatever you have — full gym, home gym, or completely bodyweight. Just tell the AI coach what equipment you have access to and it will design your program accordingly.
What is SweatIQ's age requirement? +
SweatIQ is intended for users 13 years and older. Users under 18 should use the app with parental guidance. SweatIQ is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no objectionable content.
Which AI powers SweatIQ? +
SweatIQ is powered by Claude, an AI assistant made by Anthropic. Your messages are processed by Anthropic's servers to generate coaching responses. SweatIQ does not sell your data or use your conversations to train AI models.
⚠️ Delete Your Account

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data from our servers, including your subscription and account information. Locally stored data (photos, measurements) must be cleared separately from the Progress page.

This action cannot be undone.
